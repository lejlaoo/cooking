Chocolate Cake Recipe


This is the recipe for the first delicious cake.


Ingredients:

  • 1 and 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

Instructions:

  • Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour a 9x13-inch baking pan.
  • In a large mixing bowl, sift together the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  • Add the eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla extract to the dry ingredients. Beat on medium speed for about 2 minutes until well combined.
  • Stir in the boiling water. The batter will be thin, but that's okay.
  • Pour the batter into the prepared baking pan.
  • Bake in the preheated oven for 30 to 35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  • Remove from the oven and let it cool completely in the pan on a wire rack.
  • Once cooled, you can dust the cake with powdered sugar or frost it with your favorite icing.
  • Slice and serve! Enjoy your delicious homemade chocolate cake.

Spaghetti Recipe


This is a basic spaghetti recipe.


Ingredients:

  • 8 ounces (225g) spaghetti pasta
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 can (14 ounces/400g) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional, for serving)
  • Fresh basil leaves (optional, for garnish)

Instructions:

  • Cook the spaghetti pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  • In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat.
  • Add the minced garlic and chopped onion to the skillet. Sauté until the onion is translucent and fragrant, about 3-4 minutes.
  • Stir in the crushed tomatoes, dried oregano, and dried basil. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  • Simmer the sauce for about 10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until it thickens slightly.
  • Add the cooked spaghetti to the skillet with the sauce. Toss everything together until the spaghetti is well coated with the sauce.
  • Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.
  • Serve the spaghetti hot, topped with grated Parmesan cheese and fresh basil leaves if desired.
