Recipe 1

This is the recipe for the first dish. Ingredients:

  1. 1 and 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  2. 2 cups granulated sugar
  3. 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  4. 1 and 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  5. 1 and 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  6. 1 teaspoon salt
  7. 2 eggs
  8. 1 cup milk
  9. 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  10. 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  11. 1 cup boiling water

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour a 9x13-inch baking pan.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, sift together the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Add the eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla extract to the dry ingredients. Beat on medium speed for about 2 minutes until well combined.
  4. Stir in the boiling water. The batter will be thin, but that's okay.
  5. Pour the batter into the prepared baking pan.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 30 to 35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  7. Remove from the oven and let it cool completely in the pan on a wire rack.
  8. Once cooled, you can dust the cake with powdered sugar or frost it with your favorite icing.
  9. Slice and serve! Enjoy your delicious homemade chocolate cake.
